# Shipping Information

## Standard Shipping
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days and costs $5.99. This is our most economical option for customers who don't need their items immediately.

## Express Shipping
Express shipping takes 1-2 business days and costs $12.99. This is perfect for customers who need their items quickly but don't require overnight delivery.

## Overnight Shipping
Overnight shipping takes 1 business day and costs $24.99. This is our fastest option, ensuring delivery the next business day.

## Free Shipping
Free shipping is available on orders over $50. This applies automatically at checkout when your order subtotal exceeds $50 before taxes and shipping.

## International Shipping
We offer international shipping to most countries. Rates vary by destination and typically take 7-14 business days. International orders may be subject to customs fees and import duties.

## Tracking Information
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's website to track your package in real-time.

## Shipping Restrictions
Some items may have shipping restrictions due to size, weight, or regulatory requirements. These restrictions will be clearly indicated on the product page.
