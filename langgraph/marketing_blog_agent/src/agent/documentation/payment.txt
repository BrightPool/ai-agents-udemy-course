# Payment Information

## Accepted Payment Methods
We accept the following payment methods:
- Visa
- MasterCard
- American Express
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Shop Pay

## Payment Security
All payments are processed securely with SSL encryption. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your payment information and comply with PCI DSS requirements.

## Billing Information
- Billing address must match the address on file with your payment method
- International orders may require additional verification
- Some payment methods may have additional fees

## Payment Processing
- Orders are not charged until they ship
- You will receive an email confirmation when payment is processed
- Payment authorization may be held temporarily for verification

## Gift Cards and Store Credit
- Gift cards can be used for any purchase
- Store credit is applied automatically at checkout
- Gift cards do not expire
- Gift card balances can be checked on our website

## Failed Payments
If a payment fails:
- Check that your payment information is correct
- Ensure sufficient funds are available
- Contact your bank if the issue persists
- Try an alternative payment method

## Refunds and Credits
- Refunds are credited to the original payment method
- Processing time varies by payment method (typically 3-10 business days)
- Store credit is available immediately
- Refund confirmations are sent via email

## International Payments
- Additional fees may apply for currency conversion
- Some payment methods may not be available in all countries
- Customs duties and taxes are calculated separately
